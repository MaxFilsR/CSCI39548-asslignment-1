Marty the Zebra

Striped equids found in Africa's grasslands and savannas.

Each zebra has a unique stripe pattern.

Herbivores, feeding mostly on grasses.

Live in groups called harems or larger herds.

Stripes may help confuse predators or regulate body temperature.
