Alex the Lion

Native to Africa, lions are large carnivorous cats.

Males usually have a mane, which makes them stand out.

They live in social groups called prides.

They are apex predators, feeding on animals like antelopes, zebras, and buffalo.

Known as the "king of the jungle," though they mostly live in savannas and grasslands.
