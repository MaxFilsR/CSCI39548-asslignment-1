KingJulian the Ring-tailed Lemur

A primate species native to Madagascar.

Known for their long, bushy, black-and-white striped tails.

They are social animals, living in groups called troops.

Mostly eat fruit, leaves, and flowers.

Spend a lot of time sunbathing with arms stretched out.
