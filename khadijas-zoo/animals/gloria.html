Gloria the Hipo

Large, semi-aquatic mammals found in rivers and lakes across sub-Saharan Africa.

They spend much of their day in the water to stay cool.

Despite their bulky size, they can run surprisingly fast on land.

Herbivores that graze on grasses at night.

Known for being highly territorial in water.
