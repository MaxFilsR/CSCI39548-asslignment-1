Teddy the Bear

Teddy the Bear is a calm yet powerful presence at the zoo, often seen strolling around his habitat or relaxing in the shade. He enjoys a varied diet of fruits, fish, and honey, reflecting his omnivorous nature.

Fun Fact: Bears like Teddy have an incredible sense of smell—seven times stronger than a bloodhound’s!
